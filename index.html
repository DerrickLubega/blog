Travel Guide Uganda

Uganda, a landlocked country in East Africa, captivates with its stunning landscapes that range from vast savannas to dense rainforests and majestic mountains. The country is renowned for its diverse wildlife, including the famous mountain gorillas and chimpanzees, as well as its impressive lakes and rivers such as Lake Victoria and the Nile’s sources.
